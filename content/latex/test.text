On the Convergence of Adam and Beyond
=====================================

Recap of Adam
-------------

Before we jump into Adam’s problems, we’ll briefly recap the algorithm
and its intuition. Below is the algorithm, adapted from
<https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.6980>.

\[h!\]

\[1\] **Input**: stepsize $\alpha$, $\beta_1, \beta_2$ exponential decay
rates, $f$ objective function, $\theta$ initial parameters $m_0 \gets 0$
$v_0 \gets 0$ $t \gets 0$ $t \gets t + 1$
$g_t \gets \nabla_{\theta}f_t(\theta_{t - 1})$
$m_t \gets \beta_1 \cdot m_{t - 1} + {\left(1 - \beta_1\right)} \cdot g_t$
$v_t \gets \beta_2 \cdot v_{t - 1} + {\left(1 - \beta_2\right)} \cdot g_t^2$
$\hat{m}_t \gets m_t/(1 - \beta_1^t)$
$\hat{v}_t \gets v_t/(1 - \beta_2^t)$
$\theta_t \gets \theta_{t - 1} - \alpha \cdot \hat{m}_{t - 1} / (\sqrt{\hat{v}_t} + {\epsilon})$
**Output**: $\theta_t$
